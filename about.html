About Kenya

Learn about our rich culture, history, and people.

Culture & Heritage

Kenya is home to over 40 ethnic groups, each with its own unique traditions, languages, and customs. From the Maasai warriors to the Swahili coast, Kenya’s cultural diversity is a source of national pride.

History

Kenya’s history is as diverse as its landscapes. From ancient trade routes to the struggle for independence, Kenya’s past shapes its vibrant present.

People

Kenyans are known for their warmth and hospitality. The spirit of “Harambee” (pulling together) is at the heart of Kenyan society.
